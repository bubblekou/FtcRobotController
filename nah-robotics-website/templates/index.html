About Us

We are a local team from Palo Alto, California. Even though we are all 8-9th grade, we still can be a formidable opponent for anyone. We are a three person tryhard casual team, but all our three members are very experienced in our respective fields. We design good robots, program them to their limits, and go absolutely tryhard mode during practice runs. Our team has the right people doing the right things at the right times.
